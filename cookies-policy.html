Effective Jan, 2019

This Cookie Policy explains the use of cookies by Whole Sale Ticket Expert and its subsidiary and affiliates (referred to as "Whole Sale Ticket Expert," "we," "us," or "our") on our website www.wholesaleticketexpert.com ("Website") and Mobile Application ("Booking Platform"), as well as on partner and licensee websites with whom we have agreements (collectively referred to as the "Service").

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are downloaded to the devices you use to access and use the Service. These cookies are stored by your browser or device and are available whenever you revisit a site, allowing the site to recognize you and remember important information about you.

Session Cookies: These are temporary cookies that remain in your browser or device until you leave the site. They are then deleted from your device once you leave the Service.

Persistent Cookies: These cookies remain in your browser or device for a longer period, until you delete them or they reach their expiry date. Persistent cookies may be used to remember your travel preferences, recognize you on your return, and enhance your experience on the Service. They may be linked to personal information when you interact with the Service, such as when you choose to store your user names, addresses, contact numbers, and email IDs, so that you don't have to enter them every time you access your accounts. If you reject persistent cookies, you can still use the Service but may be limited in certain areas.

2 Can I Change My Cookie Preferences?

  • Yes, you can change your cookie preferences. Most web browsers allow you to control and manage cookies.
  • You can usually modify your browser settings to refuse cookies or to be alerted when cookies are being sent.
  • However, please note that disabling or blocking cookies may affect the functionality and performance of the Service.

3 Why do we use cookies?

We use cookies to improve the content, functionality, speed, and usability of our website. Cookies help us understand your preferences, so you don't have to provide the same information repeatedly during a visit or across multiple visits.

4 How do we use cookies?

Our website uses various types of cookies:

  • Technical Cookies: Essential for the proper functioning of the website.
  • Functional Cookies: Required to provide services requested by users on the website.
  • Analytical Cookies: Enable us to analyse how visitors use the website, track viewed content, and identify areas for improvement.
  • Social Cookies: Allow functionality related to social networks (e.g., Facebook, Google, and Twitter).
  • Affiliated Cookies: Track visits from our affiliated websites.
  • Advertising Cookies: Collect information about your preferences and choices on the site, enabling customized advertising on other websites.

5 How to Control Cookies?

You can control and delete cookies at any time. Detailed instructions for managing cookies can be found at www.aboutcookies.org. Cookie preferences must be set for each browser you use (e.g., Internet Explorer, Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, or Safari). You have various options for managing cookies, such as enabling or disabling cookies, deleting stored cookies, blocking cookies from specific websites, or blocking third-party cookies. However, please note that some parts of the website may not function properly if you disable or block cookies.
